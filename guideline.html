Guidelines

  • The equation should consist only of one variable 'x'.
  • The equation can be entered in the following forms:-
      ax^n+bx^n-1+...+c=0
      ax^n+bx^n-1+...+c=ax^m+bx^m-2...+c
  • For higher order equations(power greater than 2) the equation should have atleast one integer root.
